  • Why silkscreen?
    Silkscreen shirts, over the long run, will run cheaper than other shirts. After paying set-up fee, a one color shirt will run you $4 per shirt. Wholesale means I will ship all shirts to you, and you will be responsible for shipping them to your viewers
  • Can I use someone else's design?
    Yes! Though, not every design is great for silkscreening. Good designs will have anywhere from 1 to 3 colors.
  • Can you make me a design?
    I charge $30 for simple, 1 color designs. 2 color or complex one color designs are $60. Complex 3 color designs run for $90. If you're not sure where you fall, just contact me.
